## Creating a Music Visualizer
 
 For this lab, you will be creating a music visualization program. The lab is divided into three sections:

 * Setup - load an MP3, and make a button to start/stop the music.
 * FFT - analyze the music with a Fast Fourier Transform (FFT)
 * Visualization - Use the array of numbers from the FFT to create a visualization that reacts to the music

 Each section has a list of criteria and an assessment to verify your work. Be sure to pass each section before moving on to the next page.
