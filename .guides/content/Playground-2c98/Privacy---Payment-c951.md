## Privacy by Default

No input (prompts, etc.) will be used by Anthropic to train their models. Most companies turn this on by default, and make you opt out of this.

No data retention policy - when a request is complete, it is deleted from their server. Standard policy is 30-day retention period. 

Only exception would be if we report an error, we would send them information for context.

## LLM Proxy

Codio provides an "LLM proxy" that sits between your students and the model. There is no need for them to create an account, authenticate, or make payments. You can use your own institution key, and we can add access to other models.

**Important**, the LLM proxy is only for accessing an API. AI-based tools covered over the next few pages do not require this feature. You will not be charged for using them.
